Outer Ring — Context Chords (16)

  • Resolve — Diatonic resolutions (V→I, IV→I)
  • Color — Modal interchange, borrowed chords
  • Tension — Tritone subs, secondary dominants
  • Portal — Distant keys, chromatic mediants

Inner Ring — Circle of Fifths (12)

  • Select root note from the circle of fifths
  • Chord quality auto-assigned by diatonic function
  • Pressing a root updates the outer ring context
  • Bright = diatonic, dim = chromatic

Small Ring — Quality Modifiers (8)

  • 7th (default) · 9th · 11th · 13th
  • sus4 · add9 · 6/9 · triad
  • Toggle to upgrade chord extensions globally
  • Active modifier glows amber on Launchpad

Center — Loop Controls (4)

  • REC — Start/stop recording chord loop
  • PLAY — Play/pause recorded loop
  • UNDO — Restore previous chord state
  • VOICING — Cycle: close, drop2, open…
